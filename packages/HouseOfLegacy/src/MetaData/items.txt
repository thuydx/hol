Prop_have
Prop_have lists all the items in your warehouse.  You can't spawn items but you can change the amount that you have. So you can just buy one item of any and change amount as needed.
The list under Value changes depending on when the item was bought but will follow what you can see when you open warehouse list or hit "v" key. Items just bought will appear at the bottom.
Grain will always appear at the top of value list followed by vegetables then meat, etc. Candles / offerings appear last before fertilizer. Item ID doesn't matter because you can't spawn new items.
